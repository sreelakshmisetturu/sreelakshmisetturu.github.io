NYC Connect

Our project aims to analyze NYC Taxi data based on trip records in Dataset. The predictions made on this data can help the taxi business to grow their revenue by redirecting their services to areas with more demand.
Predicted the area with more taxi demand in given time and day. Predicted how much surcharge will be applied when a customer requests a taxi in given time and pick up location, with 97% accuracy. Implemented Naive Bayes, Logistic Regression, K means clustering algorithms in Apache Spark and Python.
